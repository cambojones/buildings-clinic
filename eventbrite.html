Buildings Resources Clinic Series for NYC multifamily buildings (5 to 50 units)

Practical steps for small & mid-size multifamily properties

Register once. Choose the full workshop or any session.

Three focused 30-minute sessions designed for NYC multifamily owners and managers. After you register, you’ll receive the Zoom link in your confirmation email (or via the Eventbrite↔Zoom integration).

  • Session 1: Compliance Made Simple
  • Session 2: Upgrade for Comfort & Savings
  • Session 3: Building Upgrades with Confidence

Note: Zoom details are included in your Eventbrite confirmation & reminders.

Session 1

Compliance Made Simple

Plain-language overview of LL152 (gas piping), Lead Law 31, LL55 allergens, LL97/Benchmarking (if applicable), and DEP backflow requirements.

3-Step Plan: Identify what applies • Schedule inspections • Track deadlines

Session 2

Upgrade for Comfort & Savings

How to plan building system upgrades for better comfort and lower costs: Clean Heat (Con Edison/NYSERDA), DEP Multifamily Conservation Program, and HPD’s Green Housing Preservation Program.

3-Step Plan: Assess needs • Book a free assessment • Apply the right programs

Session 3

Building Upgrades with Confidence

Supportive guidance on tenant communication during upgrades, rent regulation basics, eviction rules, and financing tools (C-PACE, NYCEEC, Accelerator Marketplace).

3-Step Plan: Communicate early • Understand rent rules • Secure financing
